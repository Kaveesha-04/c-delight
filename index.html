Espresso
A bold and rich black coffee brewed with finely ground espresso beans, perfect for an energizing start.
Latte
A smooth espresso combined with steamed milk and a small layer of foam, ideal for those who enjoy a creamy coffee experience.
Cappuccino
Espresso, steamed milk, and a thick layer of foam come together to create a well-balanced and rich coffee experience.
Americano
Espresso diluted with hot water, resulting in a smooth and bold coffee flavor that’s less intense than straight espresso.

Coffee Brewing Methods

  • French Press:

    A classic immersion method that produces rich, full-bodied coffee.

  • Pour-Over:

    Allows precise control over water flow for a clean, balanced cup.

  • AeroPress:

    Quick and versatile, ideal for single servings of smooth coffee.

  • Cold Brew:

    Steeping coffee in cold water for a refreshing and mellow flavor.
